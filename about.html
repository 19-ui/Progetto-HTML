HI, I'M MARTA

Hi, I'm Marta, a creative Designer.

I was born in Turin in 1997.


After artistic studies in the High School of my Hometown, I graduated in 2019 in Fashion Design, at Politecnico di Milano.


I did some work experience in the field of Fashion during 2020, and then I decided to focus more on my passions: graphics, Illustration and UI Design. Although Fashion still remains one of my interest.


I'm a determined person, and I care about doing a good job. Also, I've always been curious about what surrounds me, and I'm always looking for inspiration everywhere.
