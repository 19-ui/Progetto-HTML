HI, I'M MARTA

Hi, I'm Marta, a creative Designer.

I was born in Turin in 1997.


After my art studies in my Hometown High School, I graduated in Fashion Design, at Politecnico di Milano, in 2019.


I did some work experience in the field of Fashion during 2020, and then I decided to focus more on my passions: graphics, Illustration and UI Design, although Fashion still remains one of my interests.


I'm a determined person, and I care about doing a good job. Moreover, I'm always very curious about what surrounds me, and I always look for inspiration everywhere.
